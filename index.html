ABOUT US

With a mission to help everyone around us and a focus on SDG 2: Zero Hunger, The Brijwasi Foundation was founded by Prisha Singh in November 2023. We are committed to making the world better through acts of kindness and holding distribution drives.
To date, we have positively impacted over 1,300 people and animals by providing essential resources such as whole grains, cooked food, water, clothes etc. Join us in our mission to eradicate hunger and create a brighter tomorrow for all. ABOUT OUR FOUNDER

Our Founder and President, Prisha Singh aspires to bring a positive change in society. She is currently in 12th Grade and is devoted to the commitments of the NGO.
Prisha has been actively involved in various social activities since her early childhood. Her passion for helping others has driven her to establish this NGO, which aims to address various social issues and uplift the underprivileged sections of society.
Under her leadership, the NGO has successfully launched numerous initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and women empowerment. Prisha's dedication and hard work continue to inspire many young individuals to join the cause and make a difference.
